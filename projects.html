This app was designed by a group of students in our intro to Software Engineering course at Simon Fraser University. The project was designed to help improve and meet the eco needs of Canada. With the signing of the newest Paris acoord, it is more important than ever to help canadians keep track of their CO2 emissions.

This project taught me the importance of using an Agile and Scrum methodologies. With weekly meetings and constant feedback of team members, it allowed us to create a complete project in a timely manner.

Poor working conditions and long hours have pushed the elves to plot a rebellion against Santa. Santa is thrown in a workshop dungeon where he has to complete the challenge in each room to unlock the next, and eventually, escape the workshop to deliver the gifts on time.

With the trend of VR gaming, me and my friends decided to work on a fun game that was mobile friendly. We were interested with the idea of implementing a game designed for Google cardboard. Being one of the cheapest choices of headsets it would be easily used by others.

With no experience of making any viable applications me and my friends decided to participate in this fun competition that allowed us to innovate an app to help your in your daily life.

From experience, it is difficult for students to keep track of their constant purchases. So we decided to create a unique and easy way to keep track of your spending.

Designed with an SQL backend database we created a simple android app. It did not turn out to be the prettiest app, however all the back end framework was done.
